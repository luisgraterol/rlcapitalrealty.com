### Property Services

| Cost | Frequency | 2BR (monthly) | 3BR (monthly) |

| Lawn care | Apr–Oct: 2x/mo · Nov–Mar: 1x/mo (~19 visits/yr) | $75 prorated | $82 prorated |

> **Lawn care** only applies to properties with an exterior yard — confirm before signing. If no yard, cost is $0.

> **Pest control** is non-optional in West Texas. Bark scorpions, American cockroaches, and fire ants are endemic in Abilene. A guest who encounters a scorpion is a guaranteed 1-star review.

### Maintenance & Administrative

| Cost | Frequency | 2BR | 3BR |
|---|---|---|---|
| Reactive maintenance (1.5% of rent, min $75 / max $150) | As needed | $75–150 | $75–150 |
| Preventive inspection (handyman) | Every 2 months (6x/yr) | $50 prorated | $50 prorated |
| HVAC filters | Every 45–60 days (6–8x/yr) | $8 prorated | $12 prorated |
| STR permit — Abilene (prorated) | Annual | $10 | $10 |
| CPA / taxes (prorated) | Annual (~$500/yr, billed in Q1) | $42 | $42 |

> **Preventive inspection:** the cleaner should have standing instructions to report any post-stay anomaly with a photo. This reduces emergency handyman calls and makes inspections more efficient.
>
> **CPA:** the actual payment is a single annual invoice (~$500) issued in Q1. The model prorates it to $42/month to smooth the cash flow impact.
